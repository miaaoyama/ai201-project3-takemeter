# TakeMeter Planning

## Community

K-pop discussion communities on Reddit, including r/kpopthoughts, r/unpopularkpopopinions, r/bangtan, and r/straykids.

## Classification Goal

The goal is to evaluate the type and quality of K-pop discussion takes, rather than whether the opinion itself is correct.

## Labels

### Evidence-Based Take

An opinion supported by reasoning, examples, industry knowledge, statistics, specific performances, lyrics, choreography, marketing, or historical context.

### Personal Preference Take

An opinion primarily based on individual taste or enjoyment. The user explains what they like or dislike but provides limited supporting evidence.

### Reactive Take

An emotional, exaggerated, dismissive, provocative, or inflammatory opinion with little meaningful reasoning.

## Dataset Plan

Collect at least 200 comments from K-pop discussion communities.

Target distribution:

* Evidence-Based Take: 70
* Personal Preference Take: 70
* Reactive Take: 60

## Expected Challenges

The most difficult distinction will likely be between Evidence-Based Takes and Personal Preference Takes when users provide some reasoning but not enough evidence to clearly support their claims.
